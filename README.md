# Capstone 3: Sales Analysis with Power BI

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data for EmporiUm, a virtual student bookstore, 
using Power BI. The focus is on the South region (Florida and South Carolina), 
covering sales performance from 2022 to 2025.

The goal of this analysis is to identify sales trends, evaluate regional 
performance, and provide insights that support business decisions such as 
inventory planning, marketing strategies, and product focus.

## Key Insights
- Florida consistently generated higher revenue compared to South Carolina
- Technology & Accessories was the top-performing category across all years
- Sales show an overall upward trend from 2022 to 2025 with notable spikes in 2024 and 2025
- A small number of products contributed to a large portion of total revenue (Top 10 analysis)

## Key Visuals
- Sales trend over time (line chart)
- Sales performance by category (column chart)
- Regional sales by state (donut chart)
- Top 10 best-selling books with authors (table)

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query (data cleaning and transformation)
- GitHub (version control)
